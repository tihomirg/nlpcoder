Two kinds of users:
1) experienced user - types a couple of words and expects long(er) meaningful output. Useful, since process of typing and memorizing right sequence is tedious.
Technique that help: Sequence extraction, sorting by frequency, sequence context in training data (variable names), sequence context in a user code.
2) inexperienced user - types a discourse and expects meaningful output. Useful, since the user never typed the sequence before.
Techniques that help: discourse to group of list of declaration's groups, anaphora resolution.
